    Career Training

    At CODE THE WEB, we don't just teach IT skills - we empower students for future careers. Our career training programs are designed to bridge the gap between academia and industry, ensuring our students are job-ready and competitive in the market.
    ● Industry-Relevant Skills: Our Training programs focus on the latest technologies and trends, ensuring students are equiped with the skills employers demand.
    ● Practical Experience: We emphasize hands-on learning, providing students with real-world experience and projects to build their portfolios.
    ● Career Guidance: Our experienced trainers offer mentorship and guidance, helping students navigate their career paths and make informed decisions.
    ● Job Placement Support We work closely with industry partners to provide job placement opportunities, intenships, and networking events.
    By Choosing us, students gain access to comprehensive career development program that goes beyond technical skills. We help them build confidence, develop a professional network, and unlock their full potential in IT industry.

    Group Work

    We foster a collaborative environment where students work in groups on real-world projects, promoting:
    ● Teamwork and Communication
    ● Problem-solving and critical thinking
    ● Shared Knowledge and expertise
    By working together, students develop essential skills, build strong relationships, and achieve common goals.

    Hands-On Learning

    Our training programs are 90% practical with 10% theory, focusing on real world projects and applications. Students gain hands-on experience, working on:
    ● Live Projects
    ● Case Studies
    ● Simulations
    This immersive approach enables students to:
    ● Apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios
    ● Develop problem-solving skills and critical thinking
    ● Build a strong portfolio showcasing their work
    ● Gain confidence in their abilities
